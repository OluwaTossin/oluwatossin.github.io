About Me

Platform Engineer | DevOps, SRE & MLOps Specialist | Problem Solver with a Systems Mindset

My journey into platform engineering began with a simple question: How can we build systems that not only run, but scale securely, enable developer productivity, and adapt under pressure?

Since then, I've spent the last few years helping startups, AI/ML companies, and enterprises answer that question by designing internal developer platforms, implementing comprehensive observability frameworks, and building secure MLOps pipelines across AWS, GCP, and hybrid environments. I've delivered self-service platforms, automated GitOps workflows, and resilient infrastructures, achieving outcomes like 30% cloud cost reduction, 40% faster deployment cycles, and sustained 99.99% uptime.

As a Platform, DevOps, SRE & MLOps Engineer with over 4 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in creating platforms that abstract complexity while providing powerful self-service capabilities. I excel at modernizing infrastructure using Kubernetes (EKS/GKE with Istio service mesh), advanced Terraform configurations, ArgoCD for GitOps, and implementing monitoring solutions with Grafana and Prometheus. My approach focuses on building platforms that are secure-by-design, observable-by-default, and enable teams to move fast without breaking things.
